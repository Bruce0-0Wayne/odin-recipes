CHICKEN TIKKA

# Description

Chicken tikka is a delectable Indian dish renowned for its vibrant flavors and aromatic appeal. Tender chunks of chicken are marinated in a rich blend of yogurt and spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. This infusion ensures a harmonious balance of savory, tangy, and subtly spicy notes. The marinated chicken is then skewered and grilled to perfection, creating a tantalizing char and smoky essence. The result is a symphony of textures, with the exterior boasting a flavorful crust while the interior remains succulent and juicy. Often served with mint chutney or yogurt sauce, chicken tikka is a favorite appetizer or main course, celebrated for its ability to deliver an authentic taste of Indian cuisine. Its popularity extends globally, making it a beloved dish that showcases the culinary richness of Indian gastronomy.

# Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • yoghurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder
  • Lemon Juice

# Steps

  1. Take a bowl and add yoghurt, ginger-garlic paste, salt, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, lemon juice and mix it well.
  2. Add chicken pieces in it and mix it well. Marinate for 2 hours.
  3. Skewer the chicken pieces and cook in a preheated oven for 10 - 15 minutes.
  4. Remove it from oven and serve hot.
