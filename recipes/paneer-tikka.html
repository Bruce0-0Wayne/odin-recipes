PANEER TIKKA

# Description

Paneer Tikka is a luscious and creamy Indian dish that tantalizes the taste buds with its rich flavors and velvety texture. Succulent paneer pieces are marinated in a mixture of cream (malai), yogurt, and a blend of aromatic spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, and nutmeg. This marinade imparts a luxurious and mildly spiced profile to the dish. After marination, the paneer is typically grilled or cooked in a tandoor, resulting in a smoky char that enhances the overall experience. The creaminess of malai, combined with the aromatic spices, creates a harmonious blend of savory and mildly sweet notes. Garnished with fresh coriander and served with naan or rice, Paneer Tikka stands out as a delightful example of Indian cuisine, offering a satisfying and indulgent dining experience.

# Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Yogurt
  • Cream
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder
  • Lemon Juice

# Steps

  1. Take a bowl and add yoghurt, cream, ginger-garlic paste, salt, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, lemon juice and mix it well.
  2. Add paneer pieces in it and mix it well. Marinate for 2 hours.
  3. Skewer the paneer pieces and cook in a preheated oven for 10 - 15 minutes.
  4. Remove it from oven and serve hot.
